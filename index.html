How to Play

Game Controls

Movement: Use the arrow keys (‚Üë, ‚Üì, ‚Üê, ‚Üí) to move your bunny around the garden.

Objective: Collect as many eggs as possible before the time runs out!

Egg Types

Regular Egg

Worth 1 point each. Comes in various colors and patterns.

Golden Egg

Worth 5 points! These rare eggs have a special golden glow.

Rainbow Egg

Worth 10 points! The rarest egg with shifting rainbow colors.

Thunder Egg

Gives your bunny a permanent speed boost (+50%)! Also worth 1 point.

Multiplayer Mode

Creating a Session: Click "Create Session" and share the link with friends.

Joining a Session: Use a shared link or enter a session ID to join.

Getting Ready: Click the "Ready" button when you're prepared to play.

Starting the Game: The host can start the game once all players are ready.

Leaderboard: Track wins across multiple games in the same session!

Tips & Tricks

  • Look for the special glow of Golden and Rainbow eggs
  • The Thunder egg's speed boost helps you collect more eggs faster
  • Navigate around obstacles carefully to reach eggs efficiently
  • In multiplayer, the player with the most eggs when time runs out wins!
